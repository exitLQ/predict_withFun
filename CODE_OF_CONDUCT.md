# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, education, appearance,
ability, nationality, ethnicity, religion, or technology choices.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact.
- Accept technical feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, sustained disruption, doxxing, and other conduct inappropriate
for a professional open-source community are not permitted.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues,
pull requests, and other contributions that violate this policy. Temporary or
permanent participation restrictions may be applied when necessary.

For sensitive conduct reports, contact the maintainer privately through the
GitHub account [@exitLQ](https://github.com/exitLQ). Do not include private
details in a public issue. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and
confidentially as reasonably possible.

This policy is informed by the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
